Supply Chain · How the Org Evolves

One company,
two futures. 同一家公司,
两种未来.

Hand a distribution company to AI and there are two distinct steps — don't conflate them. Mode A swaps each department's people for agents: automated, yes, but still the old company with a new engine — the three flows still run as a relay. Mode B is AI-native: no departments; one core optimizer treats the three flows as a single problem and solves them jointly, with agents existing only at the edges, on the interfaces. Two animated flow diagrams — watch how information moves, and where the humans stand. 把一家分销公司交给 AI,有两个台阶,别混为一谈。模式 A 把每个部门的「人」换成 agent——自动化了,但仍是「换芯的旧公司」,三流照旧接力;模式 B 是 AI 原生——没有部门,一个核心优化器把三流当作一个问题同时求解,agent 只存在于边缘接口上。两张动态流图,看信息怎么流、人站在哪里。

Mode A · Agent-Run Company

Old company, new engine: the relay pipeline 换芯的旧公司:接力流水线

The org chart stays untouched — each department's people are simply replaced by agents. The three flows still pass the baton segment by segment: one step finishes, then hands off to the next. Coordination relies on an orchestrator plus human approval gates. 组织结构原封不动,只是把每个部门的「人」换成「agent」。三流仍然逐段接力:一个环节做完,交给下一个;协调靠 Orchestrator 调度 + 人工审批闸。

Same object of management: still managing processes — agents are faster employees slotted into the same departmental boxes 管理对象不变:还是「管流程」——agent 像更快的员工,嵌进原来的部门格子
Flows run as a relay: goods wait on information, cash waits on goods — delay compounds stage by stage 三流接力:物流等信息流,资金流等物流——延迟逐级累积
Humans are the gates: PO value gate, dual-sign payments, exception escalation — people sit mid-stream in every flow 人是审批闸:PO 金额闸、付款双签、异常升级——人嵌在每条流的中段
Mode B · AI-Native Company

A new species: one core, agents at the edge 新物种:一个核心,一圈边缘

No departments. At the centre sits one coupled optimizer (a world model + an objective function); edge agents are arranged by interface, not by function. The three flows no longer relay — they are solved jointly against a single objective: cash constrains goods, goods constrain promises, all resolved in one pass. 没有部门。中心是一个耦合优化器(世界模型 + 目标函数),边缘 agent 按接口排列而不是按职能;三流不再接力,而是被同一个目标同时求解——钱约束货、货约束承诺,一次算清。

Management moves up a level: managing the objective function — the Owner doesn't approve documents, they tune weights, the way a central bank sets rates 管理对象升维:「管目标函数」——Owner 不批单据,只调权重,像央行设利率
Flows solved jointly: one incoming order resolves its promise date, its procurement and its cash impact in a single pass — no relay latency 三流同时求解:一张订单进来,承诺、采购、现金影响在核心里一次算清——没有接力延迟
Humans at the two ends only: defining what "good" means at the top, touching the physical world at the bottom — no human-shaped gates in between 人只在两端:最顶定义「什么是好」,最底接触实物——中间没有人形审批闸

These aren't two options — they're one evolutionary path. 这两张图不是二选一,是一条演变路径。

Mode A is the necessary step: trust is earned one action type at a time, and the human-shaped gates are both its signature and its value — the supply chain Action Center I'm building, and the shadow-mode pilot I'm starting at work, sit in this box. Mode B is the extrapolated end state: once coordination is engineered and every SKU can be tuned continuously, the department as a shape loses its reason to exist. The distance from A to B isn't model capability — it's two things: engineering the coordination layer, and earning organizational trust. 模式 A 是必经的台阶:信任要按动作类型一个一个赢回来,人形审批闸既是它的特征也是它的价值——我正在做的供应链 Action Center 和正要以 shadow 模式启动的真实工作试点,都在这一格。模式 B 是推演出来的终态:当协调被工程化、当每个 SKU 都能连续调参,「部门」这个形状本身就失去了存在的理由。从 A 到 B 的距离,不是模型能力,而是两件事:协调层的工程化,和组织信任的赢得。
